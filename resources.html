Resources

Resources for some topics in empirical IO, including paper replications with additional links to publicly available notes, guides, and papers.

  1. Demand estimation
    • Berry, Levinsohn, and Pakes (1995) replication: code and walkthrough
    • Other resources: IO handbook chapters on demand estimation by Berry and Haile (2021) and Gandhi and Nevo (2021), best practices for demand estimation by Conlon and Gortmaker (2020) and associated PyBLP library, practitioner's guide by Nevo (2000), BLP replication by Gentzkow and Shapiro (2016), demand estimation lecture notes by Matt Shum
  2. Dynamics
    • Rust (1987) replication: code and walkthrough
    • Other resources: IO handbook chapter on dynamic games by Aguirregabiria, Collard-Wexler, and Ryan (2021), survey of estimation methods for dynamic discrete choice models by Aguirregabiria and Mira (2010), practical guide for CCP-based methods by Arcidiacono and Ellickson (2011), online NFXP documentation manual by Rust (2000), single-agent dynamics lecture notes by Matt Shum
